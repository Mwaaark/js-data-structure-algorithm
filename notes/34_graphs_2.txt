GRAPHS

    ESSENTIAL GRAPH TERMS

        VERTEX
            a node

        EDGE
            connection between nodes

        DIRECTED / UNDIRECTED
            directions assigned to distanced between
            vertices

    TYPES OF GRAPHS

        UNDIRECTED GRAPH
            no direction to the edges
            (point both ways)

            e.g.
                Facebook friends 

        DIRECTED GRAPH
            often represented with arrows
            (could point both ways)

            e.g.
                Instagram followers

        WEIGHTED GRAPH
            with assigned value on edges


        UNWEIGHTED GRAPH
            without assigned value on edges
